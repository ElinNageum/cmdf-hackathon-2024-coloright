Learn About Color Blindness

Check out the information page to learn about what causes color blindness and how it impacts daily life!

Color blindness is a prevalent condition many people face that is not as discussed as other accessibility issues. Our Coloright team believes it is important to address the lack of awareness around color blindness and a few common misconceptions. People who have this condition are often overlooked and not properly accommodated.

Take the Ishihara Test

Test your color vision with this simple test!

The Ishihara Test is a popular and dependable self-testing activity to determine whether you may have color blindness. All you have to do is find the number hidden among dots of color!
